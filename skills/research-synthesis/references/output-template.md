# Research Synthesis Output Template

Use this template when the user asks for a synthesis brief and has not provided
a stronger format.

```markdown
## Research Question

[Question, audience, and decision this supports.]

## Key Findings

1. **[Finding]** - [What the evidence supports.]
   - Evidence: [sources]
   - Confidence: high / medium / low
   - Inference: [only if applicable]

## Contradictions Or Tensions

| Tension | Source A | Source B | Resolution |
|---|---|---|---|
|  |  |  | reconcile / preserve / needs more evidence |

## Confidence Markers

| Claim | Confidence | Why |
|---|---|---|
|  | high/medium/low | source quality, freshness, agreement, directness |

## Gaps

- [What remains unclear and why it matters.]

## Decision Implications

- [What the evidence supports doing now.]
- [What should wait for more evidence.]
```
